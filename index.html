How it works

Key Benefits

Open market lowers prices

Fully managed Kubernetes

Rich Add-Ons

Bid for cloud servers from Rackspace's large inventory of cloud capacity. Bids are fulfilled by an open market auction, within minutes.

The market price for servers is determined by the final cut-off price in the auction. Existing bids for capacity are not pre-empted unless the market price exceeds the highest price in the bid.

Leverage Kubernetes, without the complexity of managing all the moving parts. The Kubernetes control plane is fully managed, so you don't have to worry about configuring high availability, upgrades, or troubleshooting.

In addition, Spot comes out of the box with two storage classes for persistent storage (ssd and sata), a network-policy capable CNI (using Calico), and load balancers.

Bring your own favorite K8s helm charts of Operators today, while our engineers are hard at work to include these out-of-the-box, including:

  • DBaaS operators
  • Cluster backup
  • Enhanced monitoring
  • Security scanning
  • ML/Ops tooling

Open market auction

Bid for server capacity from Rackspace's large inventory of cloud capacity. Bids are fulfilled by an open market auction, and fulfilled within minutes.

The market price for servers is determined by the final cut-off price for capacity (the lowest winning bid). Existing bids for capacity are not pre-empted unless the market price exceeds the highest price in the bid.
